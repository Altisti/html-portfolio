The Best of Nordic Noir According to Liisa

My Top 3 Nordic Noir TV Series of all-time.

The Bridge

A Nordic Noir classic that has something for any fan of mysteries/thrillers. When a body is found on the bridge between Denmark and Sweden, a Danish inspector and a Swedish detective must work together to find the killer.

Bordertown

Quirky police detective, in a small town in Finland by the Russian border, delves into his memory palace to solve despicable crimes all the while trying to keep his family together.

Deadwind

The show follows Sofia Karppi, a detective of the Finnish police who is recently widowed in her 30s with two children. She returns to police work in Helsinki
